Gamlin Whiskey House - St. Louis, Missouri

Uploaded 07/26/2018

My oldest sister and I came to St. Louis to visit my other older sister, and

we decided to do brunch here. We were enjoying ourselves until a waiter spilled

an entire tray of drinks on our table, getting our phones and my sister's Tory

Burch purse soaked. He apologized and quickly cleaned up our table and belongings

with assistance from two other waiters. We found it very strange that a manager

never came to apologize for the mishap. We decided to request the manager, and we

found out that the incident was never even reported. The manager was very sweet,

and she decided to comp our bill due to the unfortunate events. Besides the incident

happening, the food was delicious, and the atmosphere was calm and friendly. Even

though my experience wasn't that great, I would still recommend coming here to eat.

Pictured is the Egg Benedict with Crab Cakes.

Dapper Doughtnuts - Dallas, Texas

Uploaded 07/04/2018

I discovered this doughnut shop on Doordash, and I immediately placed an order. I ordered

the $4.99 (6) hot mini doughnuts, and I chose the bananas foster and turtle flavors. By the

time it was delivered to me, my doughnuts were lukewarm, but the taste was still delightful.

The doughnuts were the perfect amount of sweetness, and the flavors vary tremendously. A must

try.
