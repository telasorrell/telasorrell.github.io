Dapper Doughtnuts - Dallas, Texas

Uploaded 07/04/2018

I discovered this doughnut shop on Doordash, and I immediately placed an order. I ordered the $4.99 (6) hot mini doughnuts, and I chose the bananas foster and turtle flavors. By the time it was delivered to me, my doughnuts were lukewarm, but the taste was still delightful. The doughnuts were the perfect amount of sweetness, and the flavors vary tremendously. A must try.
